# Example Runs and Agent Outputs

This directory contains real sample research reports generated by the **InvestAI** multi-agent research pipeline. These examples demonstrate the structured analysis, final decisions (INVEST or PASS), confidence scores, and individual agent outputs (financial, market, risk, overview) for three distinct companies across different industries:

## Examples Index

1. **Tesla, Inc. (TSLA) — INVEST (78% Confidence)**
   - **Industry:** Electric Vehicles and Clean Energy Storage
   - **Report File:** [`tesla_report.json`](./tesla_report.json)
   - **Key Highlights:** Strong energy storage expansion and vehicle moat offset by high near-term valuation and Chinese competition.
   
2. **NVIDIA Corporation (NVDA) — INVEST (92% Confidence)**
   - **Industry:** Semiconductors and AI Infrastructure
   - **Report File:** [`nvidia_report.json`](./nvidia_report.json)
   - **Key Highlights:** Exceptional high-conviction decision driven by near-monopoly in GPU market and CUDA software ecosystem integration.
   
3. **Apple Inc. (AAPL) — PASS (72% Confidence)**
   - **Industry:** Consumer Electronics and Services
   - **Report File:** [`apple_report.json`](./apple_report.json)
   - **Key Highlights:** High ecosystem lock-in and services growth are offset by premium P/E valuation (30x) and low single-digit revenue growth.

## Report Structure

Each report file follows the exact JSON schema defined by `ResearchReport` Mongoose model and returned by the LangGraph decision agent:

- `company`: Name of the company.
- `ticker`: Stock ticker symbol (optional).
- `decision`: Recommended action, either `"INVEST"` or `"PASS"`.
- `confidence`: Score from `0` to `100` representing the model's certainty.
- `summary`: A 2-4 sentence investment thesis.
- `positiveFactors`: Strongest arguments in favor of the company.
- `risks`: Primary risk factors.
- `analysis`:
  - `companyOverview`: Output of the **Company Agent** (overview, products, business model, competitors).
  - `financial`: Output of the **Financial Agent** (revenue summary, profitability, growth score, P/E ratio, data source origin).
  - `market`: Output of the **Market Agent** (industry trends, competitive position).
  - `risk`: Output of the **Risk Agent** (weaknesses, threats, overall risk rating).
